Introduction

What is RustPress?

More than just a CMS — RustPress is a complete content platform designed for the modern web, built from the ground up with performance, security, and developer experience in mind.

Lightning Fast

Built with Rust for unmatched performance. Handle millions of requests with minimal resources and near-instant response times.

Memory Safe

Rust's ownership model eliminates entire classes of bugs. No null pointer exceptions, no buffer overflows, no data races.

Developer First

Jinja2 templating, REST API, plugin system, and comprehensive documentation. Built by developers, for developers.

AI Integrated

The first CMS with native AI capabilities. Generate content, optimize SEO, and automate workflows with built-in AI tools.

Revolutionary

The First AI-Integrated CMS

RustPress brings artificial intelligence directly into your content workflow. Generate, optimize, and enhance your content with cutting-edge AI capabilities.

  • Content Generation — Create blog posts, product descriptions, and more with AI assistance
  • SEO Optimization — Automatically optimize meta tags, headings, and content structure
  • Image Enhancement — AI-powered image optimization and alt text generation
  • Smart Suggestions — Get real-time content recommendations as you write
